Orchestra of the Imaginary

Music ensemble Orchestra of the Imaginary was formed by Andrej in September 2021. It is an open group that performs Andrej's compositions, especially for silent movies.

Projects:

  • Andrej Goričar, Music for Young Medardus (Der junge Medardus, M. Kertesz, Avstrija, 1923, 102')
  • Andrej Goričar, Music for Erotikon (G. Machaty, 1929, Czech Republic, 89')
  • Andrej Goričar, Music for Nosferatu - symphony of horror (W. F. Murnau, Germany 1922, 95')
